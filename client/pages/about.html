About AI Resume Builder

The AI Powered Resume Builder is an advanced web application that helps users create fully formatted, professional resumes using Artificial Intelligence. This tool automatically generates clean, modern HTML resumes and converts them into perfectly aligned, print-ready PDFs.

Why Use This Tool?

  • AI generates professional resume content
  • Automatic formatting and layout organization
  • Perfect A4 PDF download without alignment issues
  • No signup or registration required
  • User-friendly for students and professionals
  • Faster than traditional Word templates

Features

  • AI-powered content generation
  • Clean HTML to PDF conversion
  • Dynamic color customization
  • Auto-save with LocalStorage
  • One-click PDF generation
  • Fully responsive design

About This Project

This project was developed as part of our Computer Science and Engineering academic work. It demonstrates the power of AI in automating resume creation, providing a fast, modern, and intelligent resume-building experience for students and job seekers.
