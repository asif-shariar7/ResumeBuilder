About AI Powered Resume Builder

The AI Powered Resume Builder is a smart tool designed to help students, professionals, and job seekers create professional resumes quickly and easily.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Save time by letting AI suggest career objectives and skill descriptions.
  • Choose from multiple professional resume templates.
  • Customize fonts, colors, and styles according to your preference.
  • Download your resume instantly as a PDF file.

Key Features

  • AI-suggested career objectives and skills.
  • Multiple resume templates (Classic, Modern, Professional).
  • Customizable font styles and accent colors.
  • User-friendly design with a step-by-step form.
